Personal Summary

As a passionate and story-focused Capture Artist, I specialise in capturing the narrative and gameplay essence of games and transforming that into cinematic language. With a strong sense of composition, pacing and sequencing, backed up with experience creating game videos, short films and professional promotional content as a freelance videographer, in conjunction with a studied understanding and passtion for narrative as a language and methodology, my expertise lies in understanding and aligning the narrative goals of game worlds with the narrative goals of trailers and cinematics through the use of cameras, music and slick editing. My experience operating in-engine tools, injectable camera systems and post production techniques, as well as handling in-game actors makes me versatile throughout the production workflow. and allows me to choreograph shots that highlight both gameplay mechanices and emotional beats, and bring those to life in the editing suite. I thrive in collaborative environments, working closely with various design teams and taking it upon myself to be familiar with their workflows, to ensure I am accurately representing their work through my visuals, and with a strong design background I am receptive.and quick to act on feedback. No matter the genre. tone or complexity, I approach each project with a true enthusiasm to engage with detail, resonate with audiences. and ensure polish in every frame.
